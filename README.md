<details>
<summary><b>⚙️ The engineering principles underneath</b></summary>

<br/>

- **Offline-first & conflict-safe sync** — the product keeps working when the network doesn't, and never loses data
- **Idempotent, financially-correct transactions** — money is never double-charged or lost, even under retries and load
- **Real-time systems** — live, responsive experiences that users trust
- **Event-driven architecture** — services stay decoupled, resilient, independently scalable
- **Multi-tenancy** — one codebase serving many customers at low marginal cost
- **Compliance & security by design** — trust, legal defensibility, reduced business risk
- **Infrastructure as Code & CI/CD** — reproducible environments, safe and frequent releases
- **Applied AI with guardrails** — real productivity gains without hallucination or runaway cost

</details>
