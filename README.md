## About

I'm a **Lead Software Engineer** with a passion for designing systems that don't just *work* — they **scale, recover, and endure**. Over the years, I've shipped products across e‑commerce, fintech, logistics and SaaS, leading engineering teams from whiteboard to production.

- Architecting distributed, event‑driven, cloud‑native platforms
- Building polyglot stacks with **TypeScript, Go and Python**
- Bridging product, design and engineering with calm, opinionated delivery
- Exploring the frontier of **AI/ML**, agentic automation and developer tooling
- Mentoring engineers and codifying the craft
